# Background Music

## Instructions

Place your romantic background music file here and name it `background.mp3`.

## Recommendations

- **Format**: MP3 (best browser compatibility)
- **File Size**: 2-3 MB maximum for fast loading
- **Duration**: 2-3 minutes (it will loop automatically)
- **Type**: Soft instrumental, romantic piano, or acoustic

## Popular Free Music Sources

- **YouTube Audio Library**: https://studio.youtube.com/channel/UC/music
- **Incompetech**: https://incompetech.com/music/
- **Bensound**: https://www.bensound.com/
- **Free Music Archive**: https://freemusicarchive.org/

## Converting to MP3

If your music is in another format (WAV, M4A, etc.), you can convert it using:
- **Online**: https://convertio.co/
- **Software**: Audacity (free and open-source)
